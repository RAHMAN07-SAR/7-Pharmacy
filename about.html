About Our Institution

Where education meets innovation

Our History & Journey

Founded in 1992, REDES Pharmacy College has grown from a small institution to one of the premier pharmaceutical education centers in the country. Our journey reflects our commitment to excellence, innovation, and the development of healthcare professionals.

1992 - Foundation

REDES Pharmacy College was established with a vision to provide quality pharmaceutical education.

1998 - PCI Approval

Received approval from Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) for B.Pharm program.

2005 - Expansion

Introduced M.Pharm programs in various specializations and expanded campus infrastructure.

2012 - Research Focus

Established advanced research centers and introduced Ph.D. programs.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Implemented smart classrooms and digital learning platforms to enhance education delivery.

2023 - Current Status

Ranked among top 10 pharmacy colleges in India with 1000+ students and 80+ faculty members.
