Pioneering Research for a Healthier Tomorrow

Advancing pharmaceutical sciences through innovation, collaboration, and cutting-edge research

Research Areas

Our research spans diverse domains of pharmaceutical sciences, addressing critical healthcare challenges

Drug Design & Discovery

Utilizing computational and experimental approaches for novel drug candidate identification and optimization.

  • Structure-based drug design
  • High-throughput screening
  • Medicinal chemistry

Pharmacology & Toxicology

Investigating drug mechanisms, efficacy, and safety profiles through in vitro and in vivo studies.

  • Molecular pharmacology
  • Safety pharmacology
  • Toxicological assessments

Nanomedicine & Drug Delivery

Developing advanced nano-carriers for targeted and controlled drug delivery systems.

  • Nanoparticle formulations
  • Targeted drug delivery
  • Controlled release systems

Pharmaceutical Biotechnology

Harnessing biological systems for the development of biopharmaceuticals and novel therapies.

  • Recombinant protein production
  • Monoclonal antibodies
  • Gene therapy vectors

Clinical Pharmacy & Outcomes Research

Evaluating drug therapy outcomes and optimizing medication use in clinical settings.

  • Pharmacoeconomics
  • Medication adherence
  • Patient safety

Natural Products & Traditional Medicine

Exploring bioactive compounds from natural sources and validating traditional remedies.

  • Phytochemical analysis
  • Standardization of herbal drugs
  • Bioactivity screening
