Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to join REDES Pharmacy College

1

Application Submission

Fill out the online application form with your personal, academic, and contact details.

  • Online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Pay application fee
2

Entrance Examination

Appear for the REDES Pharmacy Entrance Test (RPET) or submit valid scores from national-level tests.

  • RPET or national test scores
  • Online proctored exam
  • Syllabus-based questions
3

Counseling & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for counseling session and personal interview.

  • Document verification
  • Personal interview
  • Course selection
4

Admission Offer

Selected candidates will receive provisional admission letters via email and portal.

  • Provisional admission letter
  • Fee payment details
  • Document submission deadline
5

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete the enrollment process and attend the orientation program before classes begin.

  • Fee payment
  • Document submission
  • Orientation program

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various scholarships to support meritorious and deserving students

Merit Scholarship

Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance in qualifying examinations.

  • 95% and above: 100% tuition fee waiver
  • 90-94%: 75% tuition fee waiver
  • 85-89%: 50% tuition fee waiver

Sports Scholarship

For students who have represented at state, national, or international levels in sports.

  • International level: 100% tuition fee waiver
  • National level: 75% tuition fee waiver
  • State level: 50% tuition fee waiver

Economically Weaker Section

Financial assistance for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Family income below ₹2.5 LPA: 100% fee waiver
  • Family income ₹2.5-5 LPA: 75% fee waiver
  • Family income ₹5-8 LPA: 50% fee waiver
